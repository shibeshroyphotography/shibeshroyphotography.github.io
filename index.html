Introduction

Behind the Lens

I am Alex Vane, a photographer obsessed with the space between the staged and the raw. With over a decade behind the lens, I merge the storytelling of weddings with the crisp aesthetic of high-fashion editorials.

My approach is minimalist yet deeply evocative. I believe every frame should look like a still from a movie, whether it’s a bride’s quiet moment before the vow or a model finding their light.
